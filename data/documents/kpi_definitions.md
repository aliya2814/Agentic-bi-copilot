# KPI Definitions

Revenue is calculated from order item line revenue after item-level discount.

Gross margin is calculated as line revenue minus line cost. Gross margin percentage is gross margin divided by line revenue.

Month-over-month revenue change compares the latest month in the orders table with the immediately previous month.

Category performance groups product revenue by product category. The key business categories are Car Audio, 4WD Accessories, Lighting, Touring, and Marine Audio.

Margin pressure occurs when supplier cost increases reduce gross margin percentage, especially where sell price has not fully changed to offset the new cost.

Inventory risk occurs when stock quantity is less than or equal to reorder level and the product is a high revenue contributor. These products need stock priority, purchase order review, and channel allocation discipline.

Marketing impact is reviewed by comparing spend by channel with sales movement in relevant categories. June 2026 promotion activity should be evaluated alongside category revenue movement and inventory availability.
