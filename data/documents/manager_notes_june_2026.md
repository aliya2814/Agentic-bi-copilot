# Manager Notes - June 2026

June revenue improved compared with May, helped by stronger 4WD Accessories, Touring, and Lighting demand. Retail Events performed well for roof rack platforms, recovery tracks, and fit-out accessories. Google Ads also lifted Lighting demand, especially LED light bars and driving light products.

The biggest operational concern is that several strong sellers are now below or close to reorder level. Shower ensuites, roof rack platforms, LED light bars, roof top tents, component speaker sets, wake tower speakers, and marine head units should be prioritised for replenishment.

Margin pressure increased because supplier cost increases landed before all sell prices were adjusted. Touring products such as solar blankets and camp chair sets need immediate review. Some 4WD Accessories also need margin protection before the next promotion cycle.

Next actions:

- Reorder critical low-stock, high-demand products first.
- Protect margin on products with recent supplier price increases.
- Keep June marketing support for categories with profitable growth.
- Avoid discounting products where stock is constrained.
- Use document notes and database evidence together before making pricing decisions.
