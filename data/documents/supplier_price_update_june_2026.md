# Supplier Price Update - June 2026

Several suppliers advised cost increases that became visible in June 2026 trading. The main affected categories are Touring, 4WD Accessories, Marine Audio, Lighting, and selected Car Audio products.

TrailPro Touring Co increased wholesale prices on solar blankets, camp chair sets, and camp kitchen products after freight and canvas material costs rose. These products should be reviewed for margin protection because most of the cost increase has not been fully passed through to sell price.

RidgeLine 4x4 Wholesale and Outback Gear Supply reported higher costs for roof rack platforms, recovery tracks, bull bar mount kits, and other high-demand 4WD Accessories. If demand remains strong, retail pricing should be reviewed before the next reorder cycle.

NorthStar Marine Systems and Coastal Sound Imports applied smaller but broad supplier cost increases across Marine Audio, including wake tower speakers, marine head units, and waterproof wiring accessories.

Management guidance: protect gross margin on high-demand products, especially when stock is below reorder level. Use targeted price adjustments, supplier negotiation, or bundled promotions rather than broad discounting.
