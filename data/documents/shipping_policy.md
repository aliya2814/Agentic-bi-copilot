# Shipping Policy

Standard shipping uses the closest available warehouse: Sydney DC, Melbourne DC, Brisbane DC, or Perth DC. High-demand products in 4WD Accessories, Touring, Lighting, Marine Audio, and Car Audio should be allocated to the channel with the highest conversion rate when inventory is constrained.

For low-stock products, customer service should avoid split shipments unless the customer explicitly accepts delayed delivery. Products below reorder level should be flagged before confirming large Trade or Fleet orders.

Priority shipping applies to replacement items, warranty exchange items, and paid express orders. During June 2026, the operations team observed increased freight pressure on bulky Touring and 4WD Accessories, especially roof rack platforms, fridge slides, shower ensuites, and recovery equipment.

When stock is tight, Marketplace orders should not consume the last available units for high-margin products unless replenishment is already confirmed. Protect direct web, Trade, and Retail Event channels first.
